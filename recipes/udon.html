Udon

Japenese Noodle Soup

traditional thick noodles with umami filled fish broth topped with pork

Ingredients

For Homemade Broth
  • 2 1/3 cups dashi
  • 1 Tbsp Mirin
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • Pinch of Salt
For Udon Noodles
  • 2 serving of udon noodles
  • green onion for topping

Instructions

  1. In medium saucepan, add dashi, mirin, sugar, soy sauce, and pich of salt
  2. Bring sauce pan to gentle simmer, once simmering remove from heat, cover and set aside
  3. In a large pot of boiling water add udon noodles
  4. stir noodles so they wont stick
  5. once noodles are cooked drain and rinse noodles under cold water
  6. Serve noodles with broth and top with green onion
