Mi Goreng

Indonesian Noodles

Mi Goreng is a Indonesian noodle dish that is served with a sticky savoury sweet sauce

Ingredients

Noodles

  • 3 instant noodle cakes

Sauce

  • 2 tablespoonkecap manis
  • 2 teaspoon light soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon sambal oelak
  • 2 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions

  1. mix sauce in bowl
  2. cook noodles per packet instructions
  3. optional-fry an egg as topping
  4. toss noodles and sauce together
